About Me

Hello, I'm Yassine

I'm a 23-year-old graphic design student in my first year at the Académie d'Art de Carthage (AAC). With a background in computer science (Bac Informatique), I bring a blend of technical knowledge and creative vision to my work. I'm passionate about visual storytelling, exploring design through digital media, typography, and branding. Always curious and eager to learn, I'm building my skills to create impactful, thoughtful design solutions.

I specialize in logo design, iconography, poster design, and brand development. My approach combines traditional design principles with modern trends to create timeless yet contemporary visuals.
